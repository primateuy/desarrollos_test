KanbanEase: Smart Product Selection for Odoo Sales

A powerful tool that simplifies the process of selecting products using a kanban view for your sales needs.

Introduction / Features

  • Introducing "KanbanEase: Smart Product Selection for Odoo Sales" - revolutionizing how you choose products. Seamlessly integrate a Kanban view into Sale Order Lines, empowering swift, informed decisions with dynamic pricing, detailed insights, and effortless filtering. Elevate sales efficiency, one click at a time.

  • Seamless Integration: Integrate a dynamic Kanban view directly into Sale Order Lines for effortless and visual product selection.

  • Detailed Insights: Each Kanban card offers in-depth product details like name, images, descriptions, and pricing.

  • Tailored Pricing: Dynamic integration with customer pricelists ensures accurate pricing is displayed within the Kanban view.

  • Efficient Filtering: Utilize interactive filters to swiftly narrow down product options by categories, attributes, and more.

  • Instant Addition: Add selected products directly from the Kanban view to your Sale Order Line, streamlining the sales workflow.

Usage

  • Open a Sale Order.
  • Navigate to the Sale Order Line section.
  • A new "Catalog" button will be available inside the sale order line list view.
  • Clicking the button opens the Kanban view of products.
  • In the Kanban view, you can:
    Filter products using available filters.
    View product details, including prices adjusted based on the selected pricelist.
    To add a product in the Sales Order Line, simply click on its card. Alternatively, you can utilize the "+" and "-" buttons to modify the quantity. If needed, you also have the option to input the quantity directly into the text box.
